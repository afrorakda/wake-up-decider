# Wake-Up Decider
A hyper-minimalist sleep cycle calculator designed for the bedside.
One-tap decision for a better morning.

## 🚀 Concept: Decision-Free Sleep
When you're exhausted and about to hit the pillow, the last thing you want to do is calculate sleep cycles or fiddle with complex apps. This tool provides the "answer" instantly, allowing you to set your alarm and shut your eyes in seconds.

## ✨ Features
- **Instant Answer:** Opens directly to a list of optimal wake-up times based on the current moment.
- **The "15-Minute" Rule:** Automatically includes a 15-minute buffer to account for the time it takes to actually fall asleep.
- **Cycle-Based Logic:** Prioritizes 90-minute REM cycles (3 to 6 cycles) to ensure you wake up feeling refreshed, not groggy.
- **Bedside UI:** Fixed dark mode to protect your eyes and melatonin levels in a dark room.
- **Silent Feedback:** Tap a time to copy it. A subtle glowing border confirms the action without jarring "Copied!" alerts.

## 🛠 How to Use
1. **Open:** Open the tool just before lying down.
2. **Choose:** Look at the highlighted "Best" time (usually the 5-cycle mark).
3. **Copy:** Tap the card to copy the time.
4. **Set:** Paste the time into your phone's alarm app and go to sleep.

## 📐 The Philosophy
This is not a sleep tracker; it’s a **Sleep Navigator**. It doesn't care how you slept yesterday—it only cares about making your next wake-up as painless as possible. By removing the mental math of "If I sleep now, what time is 6 hours from now?", it preserves your final shreds of willpower for the act of actually sleeping.
